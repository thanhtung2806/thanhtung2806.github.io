Founders
  • Nguyen Tuan Anh

    Anh is a seasoned entrepreneur with 17 years of experience in the technology industry. As co-founder of Grab Vietnam, he played an instrumental role in building the company from scratch into a billion dollar enterprise by 2019. Anh has also been a key driver of innovation at Grab, helping the company secure the first pilot approval for "contract vehicle" in Vietnam and launching GrabBike, the first of its kind in the country and Grab's regions. He has held leadership positions at other top companies in the industry, including VinID and Grab Financial Group Vietnam.

  • Le Anh Son

    Son is a highly accomplished expert in the fields of AI, IoT, Robotics, and transportation. With a PhD in Automotive Mechanics Technology, he brings extensive knowledge and experience in automotive engineering and agriculture. Son has worked on major projects for leading organizations, including Toyota, Denson, and Japan's Ministry of Education, Culture, Sports, Science, and Technology (MEXT), where he focused on analyzing driver cognitive distraction, collecting driving behavioral data, and implementing advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). He served as CTO of Vingroup Japan, leading the development of an autonomous vehicle project with partners in Japan, before becoming CEO of Phenikaa-X.

  • Phenikaa-X

    We are proud to have Phenikaa-X, a technology company focused on AGV and autonomous vehicles, as our founding technology partner.

  • Touchstone Partners

    Touchstone Partners is a venture capital managing more than USD50 million, focus on Vietnam and South East Asia region. Its mission is to invest in start-up companies who can bring positive impact and sustainable values to Vietnam and the world. Alpha Asimov Robotics is honored to have Touchstone Partners as founding investor.
