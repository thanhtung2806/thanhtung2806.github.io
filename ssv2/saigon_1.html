Challenge

Robbie Friedman and David Schottenstein hired Neo to build out the Viewabill service connecting law firms and their clients. Neo was responsible for engineering and visual design of the Viewbill web application and iPhone app. Viewabill now provides transparency in billing for all types of hourly service providers and their clients.

In addition to the user-facing pieces of the Viewabill ecosystem, Neo integrated in many web-based hourly time tracking and billing systems. We also built out a RESTful API allowing larger, older client-server billing systems to push data to the Viewabill service.
