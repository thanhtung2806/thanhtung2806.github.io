What is Munchery?

Munchery is a whole new way to eat. Our in-house chefs create dishes that get delivered right to your door, ready to heat when you want to eat.

HOW IT WORKS

We Cook

Our chefs create dishes they love in our local kitchens. Each chef has their own style, which brings tons of variety to our daily menus.

Once it’s cooked, we chill the food to keep it fresh.

You Order

Visit our website or use our app to order. Choose from main dishes, sides, salads, desserts and drinks. Healthy and tempting kids’ meals, too. Menus change daily, so there’s always something new to try.

Meals arrive on-demand or you can schedule delivery up to a week in advance for beyond-easy meal-planning.

Food Arrives

Our friendly delivery folk arrive with your food—still chilled so it stays fresh, and packed in oven/microwave-safe containers for easy heating in about ten minutes. (We’ve been known to pop them in the fridge to heat up later, or even order extra to have the next day for lunch.)

You Enjoy

Mealtimes are more than food. They’re time to relax. Sometimes that means recharging all by yourself, other times it’s about connecting with others. We hope you enjoy your meal whether you’re eating it out of the tray in front of a screen in blissful solitude or carefully arranging it on plates to serve to your family (or anything in between).

WE GIVE

We believe everyone deserves real, nourishing food. So, every time you order, we donate to a local food bank, providing someone in need with a meal.
