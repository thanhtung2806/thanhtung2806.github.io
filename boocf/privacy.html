BLINDFOLD Privacy Policy

This privacy policy (“Privacy Policy”) describes the information collected by Blindfold Pte. Ltd. (“we,” “us,” or “our”), how that information may be used, with whom it may be shared, and your choices about such uses and disclosures. It is necessary for us to collect and to retain your personal data. If you do not provide us with your personal data, or do not consent with this Privacy Policy, we will not be able to effectively provide our services to you, if at all.

By using our websites, located at http://www.getblindfolded.com and http://www.blindfold.sg (“Websites”), our mobile app, and/or other services we provide (collectively, “Blindfold”), you are consenting to and accepting the practices set forth in this Privacy Policy. If you do not consent to our collection of your personal data or agree with any part of this Privacy Policy, then you must not access or use Blindfold.

Information we collect and how we collect it:

Information collected automatically:

When you use Blindfold, we automatically collect and store certain information about your computer or mobile device and your activities. This information includes:

  • Mobile Device ID: Your mobile device's unique ID number (e.g., IMEI).
  • Geographic Location: Your mobile device's geographic location when you first register for Blindfold through our mobile app.
  • Device Information/Specifications: Technical information about your computer or mobile device (e.g., type of device, web browser or operating system, IP address) to analyse trends, administer the site, prevent fraud, track visitor movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information.
  • Length and Extent of Usage: How long you used Blindfold and which services and features you used.
  • Cookies: We use “cookies” to keep track of some types of information while you are visiting Blindfold or using our services. “Cookies” are very small files placed on your computer, and they allow us to count the number of visitors to our Websites and distinguish repeat visitors from new visitors. They also allow us to save user preferences and track user trends. We rely on cookies for the proper operation of Blindfold; therefore if your browser is set to reject all cookies, Blindfold may not function properly. Users who refuse cookies assume all responsibility for any resulting loss of functionality with respect to Blindfold.
  • Web Beacons: “Web beacons” (also known as “clear gifs” and “pixel tags”) are small transparent graphic images that are often used in conjunction with cookies in order to further personalise Blindfold for our users and to collect a limited set of information about our visitors. We may also use web beacons in email communications in order to understand the behaviour of our customers, such as whether an email has been opened or acted upon.
  • Information you choose to provide:
    • Information We Obtain From Facebook: In order to register with Blindfold, you will given an option to sign in using your Facebook login. If you do so, you are authorising us to access certain Facebook account information, including information about you and your Facebook friends who might be common Facebook friends with other Blindfold users. By allowing us to access your Facebook account, you understand that we may obtain certain information from your Facebook account, including your name, email address, birthday, work history, education history, current city, pictures stored on Facebook, and the names, profile pictures, relationship status, and current cities of your Facebook friends. We only obtain information from your Facebook account that you specifically authorise and grant us permission to obtain.
    • nformation You Provide Directly to Us: In addition, when you register with Blindfold, you must provide certain information, including your name, email address, educational details, languages spoken, height, industry, marital status, and gender. You may provide additional information about yourself (such as your school, occupation, religion, hobbies, current city, etc.) in order to build your Blindfold profile. Any information that you provide in the non-personal identifiable section of your profile will be viewable by your match.
    • E-mails: We may require an email address from you when you register with Blindfold. We use your email for both “administrative” (e.g., confirming your registration, notifying of matches) and “promotional” (e.g., newsletters, new product offerings, special discounts, event notifications, special third-party offers) purposes. E-mail messages we send you may contain code that enables our database to track your usage of the e-mails, including whether the e-mail was opened and what links (if any) were clicked. You may opt-out of receiving promotional e-mails and other promotional communications from us at any time via the opt-out links provided in such communications, or by e-mailing lets@getblindfolded.com with your specific request. However, we reserve the right to send you certain communications relating to Blindfold which are necessary and integral to the services that are provided (such as service announcements, security alerts, update notices, or other administrative messages) without affording you the opportunity to opt-out of receiving such communications.
    • Online Survey Data: We may periodically conduct voluntary member surveys. We encourage our members to participate in such surveys because they provide us with important information regarding the improvement of Blindfold. We do not link the survey responses to your name or email address, and all responses are anonymous.

By using Blindfold, you are expressly consenting to the use of the information we collect from you for the following purposes:

  • facilitate matches with other Blindfold users;
  • respond to your comments and questions and provide customer service;
  • communicate with you about Blindfold and related offers, promotions, news, upcoming events, and other information we think will be of interest to you;
  • monitor and analyse trends, usage and activities;
  • investigate and prevent fraud and other illegal activities; and
  • provide, maintain, and improve Blindfold and our overall business

Use for Research:

In addition to the uses outlined above, by using Blindfold, you agree to allow us to anonymously use the information from you and your experiences to continue our research into successful relationships, including how to create and foster these relationships, so that we may continue to improve the Blindfold experience. This research may be published in our blogs or interviews. However, all of your responses will be aggregated, and we assure you that no personal identifiable information will be published.

  • Sharing Your Facebook Information: The number of mutual friends you have with your Blindfold matches will be shown.

You agree that you will not abuse this feature by entering the email addresses of those individuals who would not be interested in Blindfold.
