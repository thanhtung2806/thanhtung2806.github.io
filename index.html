Safety first
At Alpha Asimov Robotics, we prioritize the safety of our customers and the public. Our autonomous delivery robots are equipped with a variety of sensors and cameras to help them navigate their surroundings and avoid obstacles. Additionally, our robots are programmed to follow all traffic laws and regulations to ensure the safety of pedestrians and other road users. We also have a team of remote operators who are able to take control of the robot if necessary. We are constantly evaluating and updating our safety protocols to ensure that our robots are operating safely at all times.
