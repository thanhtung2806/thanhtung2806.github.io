How it works
Safety first
At Alpha Asimov Robotics, we prioritize the safety of our customers and the public. Our autonomous delivery robots are equipped with a variety of sensors and cameras to help them navigate their surroundings and avoid obstacles. Additionally, our robots are programmed to follow all traffic laws and regulations to ensure the safety of pedestrians and other road users. We also have a team of remote operators who are able to take control of the robot if necessary. We are constantly evaluating and updating our safety protocols to ensure that our robots are operating safely at all times.

Convenience
Convenience is key when it comes to package deliveries. That's why we've designed our autonomous delivery robots to make the process as easy and stress-free as possible for you. With our service, you can have your packages delivered directly to your location at a time that works for you. Whether you're at home or out running errands, our robots can deliver your packages safely and efficiently. Plus, you can track your delivery in real-time and receive notifications when your package has arrived, so you always know exactly where it is. And, with our smart-lock system, you don't even have to worry someone else will take your food :). With Alpha Asimov, you can enjoy the ultimate in convenience and ease when it comes to package delivery.

Green delivery
By using Alpha Asimov's electric autonomous delivery robots, customers can help reduce their impact on the environment. With optimized routes and low energy consumption, emissions are decreased and fuel consumption is reduced. The robots also promote circular economy by reducing packaging waste. Additionally, our commitment to recycle, repair and reuse materials ensures a sustainable business model. With our service, customers can have the convenience of package delivery without compromising the environment.
