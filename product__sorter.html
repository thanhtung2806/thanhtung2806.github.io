Sorter

High-speed sortation system for order consolidation and routing

The decision to invest in automation for outbound packet sortation can be daunting given the myriad of choices out there. You want to automate the packet sortation process but don’t think you have the scale for it. You wished proven sortation systems had lower TCO and faster ROI. Your life doesn’t get any easier when you think about the regular and irregular shaped packets that lack accurate dimension and weight data. Manual labour can seem to be the only and easiest way out.

We feel your pain and believe our Sorter system is the solution to reducing the outbound sortation window at your distribution and fulfilment centres. Scan your packets with 1D or 2D barcodes. Throw in a Dimensioning and Weighing module to capture your packets’ data. Add as many arms as you like, on our belt-based conveyors with primary sortation throughputs of 1,500, 3,000 or 6,000 packets per hour. Outfit your sub-sortation zones with our proprietary Pick-Put-to-Light stations.

Get in touch with us today to learn how 90% of India’s sortation market uses the Sorter system for high throughput packet sortation. Live your life easy.

Product Features

All Shapes and Sizes

Dimensioning and sortation systems have been around before us, but they handle only regular or irregular shaped packets. We built the Sorter so one size fits all.

Low TCO, Fast ROI​­

Our clients can’t stop smiling when the Sorter gives them a TCO reduction of nearly 100% and ROI of 3 - 12 months.

Continuous Parcel Induction​­

We developed our dynamic spacer to allow for non­stop regular feeding of parcels, so that proper sortation can be achieved even when parcels are half a centimetre apart.

Faster, cheaper, better​­

Scan and sort parcels of all types and sizes quickly and accurately for distribution or fulfillment at average speeds of 1,500, 3,000 or 6,000 parcels per hour.

Stop thinking, start sorting!

100% Dimensioning and Weighing​­

Humans are made for more than just measuring. Let the Sorter deal with it, be it 100 or 100,000 parcels per day.

Modular implementation​­

Our Sorter grows as your business grows. We designed the Sorter to be easily configured depending on our customers' needs and budget.

Tried and tested​
