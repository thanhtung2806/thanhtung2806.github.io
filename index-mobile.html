THE BUTLER

We designed the Butler to be the most advanced automated storage and goods-to-man order picking system in the world.

Created from the perfect union of hardware and software, the Butler is ideal for e-Commerce, logistics and manufacturing warehouse operations that deal with high-mix, high-volume order proﬁles.

No walking

Stand still and focus on order picking and inventory putting at dedicated or dual-use Pick/Put stations, while our butlers do the running around the warehouse for you.

Real-time Picking

Move beyond batch and wave picking and reduce order lead times. The Butler optimises your warehouse so you can focus on growing your business.

On­-the­-fly
ABC analysis

The Butler digests past and current order data and dynamically reslots storage racks such that fast­moving SKUs are nearer to the Pick stations.

On­ demand stock audits

Audit your stock as frequently as you or your clients want without impacting your warehouse operations.

Improved space utilisation​­

Our double­sided storage racks permit multi­face picking and reduce aisle footprint by at least 50%.

Bi­-directional growth​­

Increase storage volume and fulfillment throughput independently, by adding more storage racks or butler robots on­-demand, as­-needed; investing in excess capacity will become a thing of the past.

Intelligent charging

Our butlers perform opportunity charging so your warehouse can operate as many shifts as you desire.

The Sorter

Sorter be nimble, Sorter be quick.
With the Sorter, we didn’t reinvent the wheel; we simply made it spin faster. Dimensioning and sortation systems have been around before us, but they handle only regular or irregular shaped packets. We built the Sorter so one size fits all.

All Shapes and Sizes

Dimensioning and sortation systems have been around before us, but they handle only regular or irregular shaped packets. We built the Sorter so one size fits all.

Low TCO, Fast ROI​­

We didn’t build the Sorter to compete on looks. Instead, it performs in ways that have a direct impact on your bottom line, with TCO reduction of nearly 100% and ROI of 15 ­ 18 months.

Continuous Parcel Induction​­

We developed our dynamic spacer to allow for non­stop regular feeding of parcels, so that proper sortation can be achieved even when parcels are half a centimetre apart.

Faster, cheaper, better​­

Scan and sort parcels of all types and sizes quickly and accurately for distribution or fulfillment. 1,500, 3,000 or 6,000 parcels per hour. Stop thinking, start sorting!

100% Dimensioning and Weighing​­

Humans are made for more than just measuring. Let the Sorter deal with it, be it 100 or 100k parcels per day.

Modular implementation​­

Our Sorter grows as your business grows. We’ve designed the Sorter to help our customers add modules as and when required.

Tried and tested​­

We must have done something right.
The Sorter enjoys 90% market share in its product segment on the Indian subcontinent, and boast an installed sortation throughput in excess of 840 million parcels a year.
